WELCOME TO ILLUSTROGRAFIKA
Illustrografika is an independent duo of developers proudly based in Eastern Europe. We are passionate about making narrative CRPG games that intertwine history, culture, storytelling and art. Illustrografika is about opening and closing the doors to other times, other people and other places, however terrifying, terrible and beautiful the journey may be.

ABOUT
ROOTSOur philosophy is a radical acceptance of the simple fact - we accept that our lives - be it our cultural heritage or worldviews - will seep and creep into whatever we set our mind. However, we also vow to see the world as a diverse infinity as it is, and translate it into our with care and love for other lived experiences.
CRAFTAs a small team consisting of only two people - bedroom developers if you will - we see our work more like craft and less than production. Every element, no matter how small, is painstaking and intentional, forming a harmonious structure where every action, detail and emotion matters.
POISEToday, the world ends. Gentle moonlight illuminates a serpent opening it’s jaw, a man who has no mercy for another, a dog lashed one too many times. Don’t worry. Tomorrow, pick up the pieces and try again.
