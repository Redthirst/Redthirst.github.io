Vivace is a small coffee shop located in the Old Town of Bratislava. It’s a place that is more than just coffee to-go. We built Vivace as a cozy community space filled with warmth, character and good vibes. Whether you come alone or with friends, you’re always welcome to a hot cup of coffee.

Responsibility

WE USE FAIR TRADE
COFFEE

We respect the people behind the coffee. As part of our commitment to fair and ethical coffee sourcing, we only work with farmers and partners who guarantee respectful conditions and fair pay for the staff.

WE CARE ABOUT
NATURE

If you need a coffee on the go, we always provide you with a compostable cup. We strive minimize our environmental impact in all parts of our operation, from waste reduction to energy and water consumption.

WE DO RIGHT BY OUR
PEOPLE

Since the very beginning, we wanted to create a fair, enjoyable and safe working environment for our staff. Our employees are guaranteed fair and liveable wage because we want our team to feel valued and cared for.
